Dosa

2 cups of rice
1 cup of dal
¼ cup of channa dal
¼ cup of methi seed
Salt

After washing the rice, Soak the dals and methi seed all night or 6 hours. Put them in the blender add a little water, blend them to a fine paste. Then add salt after fermentation.

To make a dosa. Heat a pan, spread a small bowl of the liquid, spread it with the bowl in a circular motion. Add a little oil, take a spatula and rub it in the surface. For paper dosa you scrape the excess batter.
Add the potatoes in the middle and roll.

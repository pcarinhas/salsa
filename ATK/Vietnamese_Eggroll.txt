Vietnamese Eggroll

4 lbs of pork make 5 dozen eggrolls
For every 1 lbs of pork use 2 lbs of vegetables

Jicama slice thin
Taro shredded
green onion
fungus - soak
bean thread -soak in warm water cut short
1 egg

Marinate the pork with oil, garlic powder, onion powder, fish sauce, pepper, bouilon, salt, sugar, set aside. Cut the vegetable while the pork is marinating.

Mix the cut vegetable, bean thread and fungus and the pork. Add oil, garlic powder, onion powder, fish sauce, pepper, bouilon, salt, sugar and egg.

Start wrapping.

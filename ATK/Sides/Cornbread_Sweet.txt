
INGREDIENTS
1 cup buttermilk
2 eggs
2/3 cup honey
4 Tbsp unsalted butter melted
Whisk all the ingredients so honey dissolved, set it aside

6 1/4 oz flour
5 oz cornmeal stone ground
1 tsp bk powder
1tsp salt
1/4 tsp bk soda
whisk it to combine

Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mix them till combine (don’t over mix)

Pour it to 8“ pan that has been buttered. Put in the middle rack. Bake 375 F for 30 minutes or till done.

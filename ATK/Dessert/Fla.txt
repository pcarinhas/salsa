Fla

3 cups of milk
½ cup sugar, a little less than 1/2 cup maybe 1/3 cup
1 Tbsp cornstarch or 1 ½ Tbsp
a little salt
3 Tbsp Rum
2 egg yolks

Bring milk and sugar to a simmer. Temper the egg yolks with hot milk mix. Put the tempered egg yolk to the milk mixture pan not on the fire. Simmer add a little salt, rum and cornstarch.

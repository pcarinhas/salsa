Ingredients
    • 6 mangoes, about 5 to 6 cups diced or sliced (I used 4 cups sliced mango and ½ cup mango blended in the blender)
    • 1/4 cup sugar (use less)
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon, divided
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
    • 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
    • 1 heaping tablespoon cornstarch
Steps to Make It
Preheat the oven to 350 F
Butter a 1 1/2-quart baking dish.
Put the sliced mango in a bowl. Put it aside.
Put the blended mango in a pan, add lemon, cinnamon, lemon juice, cornstarch and vanilla. Heat it up till thicken.
Add the thicken mango to the bowl of sliced mango.
Put it in the baking dish (pie plate).
Add the crumble from the ATK Apple Crisp recipes. 
Bake for 40 – 45 minutes. Cover with aluminum foil half way.

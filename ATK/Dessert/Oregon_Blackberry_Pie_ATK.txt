Oregon Blackberry Pie
Crust
3 Tbsp sour cream
1/3 C ice water
2 ½ C flour
1 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 Stick of butter (16 Tbsp), froze for 15 min cut 1/4”

Combined sour cream with water.
Put flour in food processor, sugar, salt, pulse for 5 seconds. Add butter, pulse
till butter is small pieces. Add ½ of the liquid, pulse for 10 seconds and add the rest of the 
liquid.
Use plastic wrap to make 2 disks. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Filling
¾ C sugar
¼ tsp salt
4 C berries
3 Tbsp corn starch
2 Tbsp lemon juice

Combine all the filling ingredients, put it on top of the pie dough, add 2 Tbsp
cut butter.
Brush egg wash on the pie dough.

Lined a parchment paper on the baking sheet, Heat oven to 400 F, put the pie on the
lower part of the oven, bake for 40-45 minutes.

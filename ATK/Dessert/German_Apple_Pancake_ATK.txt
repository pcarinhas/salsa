German Apple Pancake

1 ¾ c All purpose flour
2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp lemon zest
½ tsp salt
1 tsp nutmeg
1 ½ c milk
6 large eggs
1 ½ tsp vanilla
3 Tbsp butter

Mix flour, sugar, lemon zest, salt and nutmeg until incorporated.
Whisk milk, eggs and vanilla till combined.
Pour 2/3 of the wet ingredients to the flour mixture till combined then add the rest of the wet 
ingredients.
Heat 3 Tbsp butter over medium low heat in 10” skillet. After the butter is melted, pour the batter.
Put it in the oven and turn the oven to 375 degrees. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Filling
1 ¼ lbs Braeburn apples, cored and peeled (cut in half, the cut crosswise then cut to 1/2” size)
2 Tbsp butter
1/3 c water
¼ c brown sugar
¼ tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp salt

Melt butter over medium heat, add water, brown sugar, cinnamon, salt, whisk till disolved.
Add the apples, bring it to simmer over medium high heat the cover it and cook for 5 minutes.
Reduce heat and cook for another 5 – 7 minutes.

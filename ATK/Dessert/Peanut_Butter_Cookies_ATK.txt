
Make 24 cookies

Ingredients
7 ½ oz all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
6 oz brown sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter
2 Tbsp honey
1 tsp vanilla
4 Tbsp melted butter
2 eggs
½ cup roasted peanut (chopped finely)

Heat oven to 350 F

1. Combined flour, baking soda, salt in a bowl, side aside
2. Whisk brown sugar, peanut butter, honey, vanilla, melted butter, eggs until combined
3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet peanut butter mixtures (wet ingredients)
4. Use a spatula to combine
5. Add the chopped roasted peanut
6. Scoop a table spoon of the dough, make a ball and press it to 2” diameter (1/4" thick)
7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, rotate them half way through.....(about 14-15 minutes)

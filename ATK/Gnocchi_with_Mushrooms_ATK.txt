1 lbs cremini mushroom (cut the end and cut to quarter)
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 ½ cup of vegetable broth
½ cup of cream
1 cup of parmesan cheese
1/8 tsp nutmeg
Black pepper
Frozen gnocchi

1. Whisk broth, cream, cheese, nutmeg, pepper, set aside
2. Heat 2 tbsp butter to brown the gnocchi about 5 minutes, set aside
3. Heat 1 tbsp butter on medium heat, cook the mushroom, add salt for 10 minutes, add garlic cook for 30 seconds
4. Mix the broth mixtures, add to the mushroom then add the gnocchi bring to a simmer then cook for 5-10 minutes

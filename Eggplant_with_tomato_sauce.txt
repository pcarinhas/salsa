Eggplant in tomato sauce

Cut eggplant into 1inch cubes, fry them till brown.

Heat oil, add whole dried red pepper, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, chopped onion, curry leaves, 
green chilies, add a little ginger garlic paste, mix it add a little turmeric add chopped tomatoes 
add 11/2 tsp coriander pdr and chili pdr cover with lid. If mixture a little dry add a little water. 
Toast some sesame seeds and peanut, ground it.
Add fried eggplant to the mixture, cover for 15 min add peanut mixture.
Immediately turn off the flame. Add cilantro.

Main Ingredients

8 oz pork shoulder butt (sliced thin and then square) - soak for 10 minutes
4 oz shrimp (peeled and deveined)
4 pieces dried shiitake mushroom 
20 pieces siu mai wrappers (can also use wonton wrappers, more on this later)
0.50 oz carrot (minced for garnish)
4 oz water chestnut sliced and diced small

Marinade
1 tbsp light soy sauce
2 tbsp oyster sauce
0.50 tsp salt
2 tsp sugar
2 tbsp cornstarch (I used 1 1/2 Tbsp cornstarch)
0.50 tsp baking soda
1 tsp chicken bouillon
2 tbsp water ( I used 1 Tbsp water and 1 egg white)
0.50 tsp white pepper
1 tsp olive oil ( I skip this)
1 tbsp sesame oil

Marinade for 4 - 8 hours
Boil steamer, steam for 6 minutes, if frozen steam for 10 minutes. Put parchment paper on the bottom to avoid sticking.

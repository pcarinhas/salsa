Puding:

- 900 ml susu cair 
- 95 gr gula pasir 
- 1 bungkus agar-agar 
- 1 1/2 sdm coklat bubuk 
- 80 gr dark chocolate 
- 1/2 sdt vanilla 
- 2 cubit garam

Melt the dark chocolate.
Mix all the ingredients, cook on low heat until boil.
Strain it, let it rest for 15 min till warm.
Pour to the cake pan, leave it in refrigerator. 

Saus Vla:

- 300 ml susu cair 
- 40 gr gula pasir 
- 12 gr maizena 
- 1/2 sdt rhum bakar 
- 1/2 sdt vanila

Mix all ingredients, cook on low heat. Keep mixing till almost boil then turn off the heat and strain it.
Put plastic wrap on top of the puding, let it rest till cool. Put it in refrigerator.
Mix it before put it on top of the slice puding.

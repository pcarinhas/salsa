40 gr milk 
75 gr water
50 gr egg yolk (3 butir)
250 gr bread flour
25 gr milk powder
50 gr gula pasir
6 gr yeast
5 gr salt (1/2 tsp)
50 gr butter
1/4 c mashed potatoes (if make double 1/4 c + 2 Tbsp) - optional, not in original recipe

Combined milk and water together, heat it in microwave for 20 second.

Mix flour, milk powder, sugar, yeast, salt, butter, mashed potatoes and egg yolks in the stand mixer on low until slightly combined, add warm liquid (mix on medium then high). 
If omit mashed potatoes then add the butter (little by little) after mixing all the ingredients. 
It will be slightly sticky (it's ok).

Let it rest for 1 hour (double in size). Ambil adonan 50 or 40 gr each, bunder2 let it rest for 10 min.
Banting 1 or 2 times, flattened it and put chocolate in the middle. Cover with plastic, rest for 40 min. 
Kocok 1 telur + 2 Tbsp susu. Oles dengan telur yg sudah dikocok diatasnya. Bake 350 F for 20-25 min. Turn half way so it will bake evenly.


Isi Roti Ayam

1 tsp salted soy sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp chicken bouilon
1/4 tsp white pepper
5 cloves shallot di rajang
1 garlic di rajang
500 gr minced chicken
100 ml water
1 Tbsp water and 1 Tbsp cornstarch for thickenig

Combined the first 8 ingredients in a small bowl.
Tumis shallot and garlic till lightly golden, add the chicken and sauce add the water, simmer till water is absorbed.

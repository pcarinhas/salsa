Heat oil add garam masala( javentri/mace, cloves, gr cardamom, bayleaves, blck cardamom, cinammon), bclk cumin seed, add chopped onion. 
Add salt, little ginger garlic paste, peppercorns, chopped gr chilies, chopped mint and cilantro. 
Add a little red chillie, little cumin pdr, very little coriander pdr, potatoes. 
After saute the potatoes add carrot and green peas cooked for 2 minutes, add small amount of yogurt.

Soak rice for 30 min, remove a little water from the rice, add the veg mixture. Cook in the rice cooker.

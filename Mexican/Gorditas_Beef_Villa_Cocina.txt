Ground Beef Gorditas Recipe

1lb ground beef, sirloin (90% lean)
2 tsp ground onion
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 1/2 Tbsp ground ancho chile
1 roma tomato
1 cup mixed veggies
1 cup chicken or beef broth, low sodium
1 tsp salt
Ground black pepper to taste
2 Tbsp olive oil

Gordita
2 cups masa harina
1 1/2 cup water plus more if needed ( I used 1 1/4 cups)
1 cup cotija cheese
1 large russet potato (I used medium size about 309 gr)....nxt time will use 200 gr

Sour Cream

INGREDIENTS 
4 C. Fresh corn off the cob 
1 14oz. can Sweetened condensed milk 
3 extra large eggs 
2 tsp Baking Powder 
7 Tbs All purpose flour 
1 1/2 tsp Vanilla 
4 1/2 Tbsp soft unsalted butter 
1/4 tsp Salt 

TIP: you can use 2 cans of canned corn (drained) or 4 C. thawed frozen corn instead of fresh. â€¢ About 40 minutes if using a 9x13 dish

1. Blend 3 cups of corn, condensed milk, butter, vanilla. Blend it real well.
2. Add 3 eggs, bk powder, salt, flour. Blend it real well.
3. Add the rest of the corn, mix it.
4. Butter the casserole dish.
5. Pour the mixture, bake 350 degrees for 45 - 1 hour. Let it cool down before you cut it.

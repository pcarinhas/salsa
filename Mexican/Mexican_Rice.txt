MOLCAJETE INGREDIENTS 
2 GARLIC CLOVES 
1/2 tsp salt 
1/4 tsp peppercorns 
1/4 tsp whole cumin
1/2 water (I used 1/4 cup)

BLENDER INGREDIENTS 
2 ROMA tomatoes (core removed) - I used 1 1/2 medium tomatoes
1/4 small onion 
spices from molcajete 

RICE 
2 TBSP vegetable oil 
1 C. Rice (I like jasmine) (rinsed and drip dry) 
1 Tbsp chicken bouillon - I used 1 tsp
1/4 C. chopped carrots (optional) 
Cilantro
2 C. water (I used 3/4 cup, add a little more water (1/2 of 1/4 cup if using iron pan)
1 serrano pepper (optional) 
mixture from blender 

In a pan add 2 Tbsp of oil, add the rice. Stir it till it golden brown. 
Add the tomatoes mixture, carrot, chicken bouillon, cilantro, serrano pepper.
Add 2 cups of water. Stir it.
Bring to a boil, then lower to simmer for 15-18 minutes (mine is 18 minutes) 
DO NOT REMOVE LID then set aside for 15 minutes and it'll be ready to serve. ●

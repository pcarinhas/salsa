INGREDIENTS----------------------------- 375Â° 20 Minutes covered and last 10 minutes uncovered (or till bubbly) 
1 lb. 80/20 Ground beef (drained ) 
2Tbsp Butter 
1 Tbs Canola (or your favorite) 
3 Tbsp All purpose flour 
1 1/2 Tbsp Chili powder 
2 tsp Paprika 
1 tsp Onion powder 
2 tsp Garlic powder 
1/2 tsp Ground cumin 
1/2 tsp Mexican Oregano 
1 1/2 tsp Chicken Bouillon 
2 tsp Tomato paste 
Salt and pepper for beef 
3 1/2 C. Chicken broth 
10 Corn tortillas or as needed 
1 lb Freshly grated American Cheese or other 

- Fry the tortila till bubblely, wipe the excess oil
- Heat oil, butter and add the flour. Stir it constanly till the flour is golden brown
- Add chili powder, paprika, mix it around.
- Add onion powder, garlic powder, ground cumin, oregano. Mix it well 30 second
- Add a little chicken broth, 1/2 tsp sugar, tomato paste, chicken bouillon. 
- Add some more of the chicken broth, a little at a time till it finished.
- Continue to mix till a little gentle boil and thickened a little.
- Add 3 spoons of the beef.
- Ready to assemble

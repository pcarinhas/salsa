1 1/2 cups Maseca flour 
Pinch salt 
1 1/4 cups warm water (APPROX more or less) - I used about 1 cup

Knead very well until it looks like playdough
Cover bowl with saran wrap and let sit for 30 minutes or more 
Make little balls (egg size) 
Press 1 at a time in tortilla press 
Cook medium high for 1/2 minute Flip and cook 1 and 1/2 minutes, press lightly till it bubbles up 
Let it cook for 1/2 minute and remove

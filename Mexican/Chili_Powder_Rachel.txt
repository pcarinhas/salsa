INGREDIENTS
4 med/large Chile Guajillo (rinsed,pat dry and air dry) - 24 hours
4 med/large Chile Ancho (rinsed, pat dry and air dry) - 24 hours
2 Tbsp whole Cumin seeds (I used 1 Tbsp and 1 tsp)
1 1/2 Tbsp Onion powder 
2 Tbsp Garlic powder 
1 Tbsp Mexican Oregano 
1 1/2 tsp Sweet Paprika 

Heat oven to 175 F, put the peppers on a cookie rack. Bake for 30 minutes, then flip them over.
Bake for 2 hours or till crispy. Break them apart and blend them.
Heat up a small pan and put the cumin seeds, stir them on low heat until they smells good. Turn off the heat.
Grind the cumin seeds, ground dried chili. Add the oregano and grind it again. Sift it to a measuring cup.
Combine all the ingredients. Sift it again to a jar.

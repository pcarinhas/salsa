Indian Butter Chicken

3 Plum tomatoes cut into small cubes

Hot the pan add butter, add green cardamom, mace a couple, add chopped garlic add the tomatoes.
After the tomatoes soft, cool a litte put in the blender--puree

While it cooking, slice some fresh ginger (juliene), slice some green chili (remove the seeds and stem) cut juliene style.

In the hot pan put butter, add slice ginger and green chili, saute for a few second, drain/ strain the tomatoe puree into the pan. Cook for a few minutes add red chili powder, salt. After 5 minutes add tandoori chicken, add fresh cream, green cardamom powder and some honey and dried fenuk greek leave powder.

Lamb Briyani

Put the lamb in the bowl add meat tenderizer—1 hr
Add green chili, salt, chili powder, blck cumin, cilantro, mint, coriander powder, cumin powder, half fried onion, crush pepper, mace, blck cardamon, green cardamon, cloves, lemon juice 27/2 cup yogurt, add oil.

Soak rice for 30 minutes.

Boil water add oil, rice, salt, shai jerra. Cook rice 40% done and the rest for 60% done.

Add marinated lamb to the pan add 40% lamb, coriander leave, mint leaves, fried onion, add 60% rice, fried onion, garam masala powder, cilantro & mint leave, safron water. Cook for 30-40min

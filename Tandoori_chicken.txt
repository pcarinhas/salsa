Tandoori Chicken

Chicken – make slits on the chicken
Put red chili powder, ginger garlic paste, lemon juice mix with the chicken----marinate at least 20 minutes
second marinate ---  han yougurt (yogurt put in the cheese cloth to reduce water), garlic ginger paste, red chili powder, garam masala powder, salt, black salt, mustard oil 2 tbsp (mustard paste add oil for substitute).

Put the chicken in the second marinate for at least 30-1 hr.

Preheat the oven, cook for 18 minutes, brush with butter. Cook another 5 minutes


Tandoori Chicken Vah reh vah

Cut chicken into quarter and breast.
Make some slits on the thickest meat part.
Combined salt, paprika, lemon juice.
Rub all over the chicken, inside the slits that you did. Rest for 15 minutes.

Combined 6 Tbsp of yogurt (1 Tbsp for each pieces), add a quarter tsp of turmeric, 1 Tbsp of salt, 1/2 of lemon juice, 
1 tsp ground coriander, 1 Tbsp ground cumin, ginger garlic paste, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1/2 tsp fenugreek leaves (crushed)
1/2 tsp garam masala, add some coloring (I grind some red yeast rice).

Add the chicken pieces to the marination, let it rest for at least 6 hours or overnite.

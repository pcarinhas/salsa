Beef Brocoli

8 oz of beef (sliced into thin pieces)

Marinate the beef:
1 Tbsp chinese cooking wine
2 tsp light soysauce
1 tsp cornstarch
½ tsp black pepper
¼ tsp baking soda
1 tsp oil

For the stir Fry:
1 cup of broccoli
1 Tbsp minced garlic

For the sauce:
1 Tbsp oyster sauce
1 Tbsp light soy sauce
1 tsp dark soy sauce
2-3 tbsp water
2 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp ground black pepper

Blanch the broccoli in boiling water for 1-2 minutes. Turn off the heat and put it into ice water for 3-5 minutes. 
Take it out and shake off the excess water.

Heat up 1 tbsp of oil, fry the beef for 15-20 second per side. Set aside.

In the same wok, add a little bit of oil. Put in some garlic and give it a stir. Pour the sauce into the hot wok. 
It will start thickening quickly. Then add the broccoli. 
Mix it a little bit. Add the beef back into the wok.

Mango Mouse

Mango pulp – 1 cup
White chocolate chopped – 1 cup
Fresh cream - ½ cup
Whipping cream - ½ cup
Chocolate chips as required

Non stick pan add the fresh cream, boil a little turn the fire off.
Add the hot fresh cream to the white chocolate until all the white chocolate melted set aside.

Whipped the whipping cream add powder sugar, whip with mixer until fluffy.

Add fresh mango pulp to the fresh cream, mix them. Then fold the whip cream slowly. Refrigerate for 15 minutes, add some mango pulp on top and chocolate chips.

Tomato Pulao

Red onion
2 Plum tomatoes
Left over rice

Chopped onion and tomatoes

Heat oil, add ginger garlic paste, add onion cook till soft and translucent 30 second.
Add tomatoes, let it cook, add ½ cup tomato ketchup, ½ tsp turmeric, 1stsp red chili powder, 2 tsp pav bhaji masala. Add green pepper, 2 green chilies stir add some water, paneer cubes, salt, add rice. Reduce heat. Add coriander leaves, last add frozen peas.

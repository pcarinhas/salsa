Galbi/Kalbi Short beef ribs

4 lbs of short beef ribs
1 cup of soy sauce
2-3 stalks of green onion
small round onion
1 tbsp of minced garlic
1 tsp of minced ginger
1 can cola
1-2 tbsp of sesame oil
1 tbsp of toasted sesame seeds
1tsp of ground black pepper
¼ to ½ sup of sugar (brown or white)
1 tbsp of sweet rice cooking seasoning

Rinse the short beef ribs, removing access fat, rinse again, then drain.

Puree all the vegetable into blender mix all the ingredients. Dip the short beef into the mixture.
Put them in the container, pour the sauce into the container to marinade.
